KNOW

Gardening is not written in stone. What works for you may not work for someone else. The Kentucky Gardener tries its best to garden with an environmentally friendly approach in mind. That means no pesticides and only natural fertilizers and fungicides on plants meant for consumption. This doesn't mean our way is the right way, and certainly not the only way, but we do our best not to contaminate the environment any more than it already is. Read on to find links to several products that will help you grow a bountiful garden in the safest way possible.
